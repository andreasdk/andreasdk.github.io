OpaleNet’s site.

OpaleNet,

2020.

OpaleNet’s website needed an urgent change. The original design looked dated and unattractive.

One of the main points in this redesign was to reflect the spontaneity, familiarity and confidence of the company, that is why I rescued the colors of the logo and used them as a basis for the design, managing to create the feeling of closeness and ease.
