uZen App.

OpaleNet,

2021.

uZen is a well-being community app that was born from the need to expand the market and reach a specific niche that developed exponentially during the pandemic period.

The design had to be simple, friendly and somehow convey calm and well-being. That’s why a pastel color palette and easy-to-understand illustrations were used.
