Divas.

Digital Illustrations.

Personal Project.

2019.

Women have taught me a lot, they have taught me to believe, to create, to love and to be brave among other things. That’s why I wanted to start an illustration project to rescue those women who have been an example and a role model in my life.

Cinegraphist.

Digital Illustrations.

Personal Project.

2019.

One of the passions of my life is and will be cinema, I grew up discovering new worlds and letting myself be carried away by the fascination it produced in me. So much is my passion that I easily remember names, dates, actors and many details of each and every one of the movies I’ve seen. It is for this reason that I decided to pay homage to those films that marked my life and that remained etched in my memory. That is what this project is about, rescuing the cinema that has made me the person I am.

Meet Market.

Digital Illustrations.

Personal Project.

2015.

Meet Market is a project that was born as a way to understand the dynamics of Grindr, a dating app for people from the LGBTI+ community. I decided to recreate the photos I saw in the app using myself as a model and then redrew the images.
