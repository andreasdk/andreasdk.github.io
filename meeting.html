OpaleNet,

2022.

OpaleNet needed to develop a white label dating site to be used with different proprietary brands each with specific niches. The idea was that the design was functional and easy to adapt to the needs of each brand.

This is how I developed a design system based on color palettes, fonts, components and other elements necessary to create an image of its own for each brand and niche.
