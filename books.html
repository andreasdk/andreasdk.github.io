Por el Otro Lado.

Amazon.

2017.

Por El Otro Lado is a personal project that I developed in the form of a book. It is the compilation of a personal blog in which I documented a year of my life. A whole process of acceptance and self-discovery.

Sexo entre Líneas.

Editorial ITA.

2019.

Sexo Entre Líneas was a collaborative two-handed writing project. The idea was to write stories related to sex and that were concatenated by some element contained in the previous story. The result was an anthology, an exquisite cadaver full of erotic texts.
